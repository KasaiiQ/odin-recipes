Butter Tofu


Butter Tofu is the vegetarian or vegan version of Butter chicken, a popular indian dish which is easy made and tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar<
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • fresh ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package extra-firm tofu, cut into 1/2-inch cubes

Directions

    Step 1

    Mix vinegar, ketchup, peanut butter, soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, ground
    ginger, cayenne pepper, and black pepper together in a small, microwave-safe bowl.

    Step 2
    Heat vinegar mixture in microwave oven until the peanut butter is softened,
    10 to 15 seconds; stir mixture until

    Step 3
    Put tofu into a large bowl. Pour sauce over the tofu and stir to
    Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 30 minutes to a full day,
    stirring occasionally to assure tofu remains coated in sauce.

    Step 4
    Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

    Step 5
    Spread tofu onto a baking sheet in a single layer.

    Step 6
    Bake in preheated oven until slightly browned, 10 to 15 minutes.
